FAQ - Frequently Asking Questions

Emulator GUI hotkeys always shown at the bottom of the window

How to change screen scaling?

Just drag the corner of the window to resize screen window. Screen are resized by steps like: 1/1 (100%); 1/2 (50%); 1/4 (25%); ... If you want to SCALE above 100% - you need to press Shift key while dragging

I get a weird error saying something about an inconsistency detected by ld.so

That's a known problem with our dependency, LWJGL2, on Java 9 and newer. As a workaround, launch Ocelot Desktop on Java 8.
